# Premium Coffee Co Sales Analysis Report
![Header Image](coffeeco_header.jpg)

## Executive Summary
Premium Coffee Co required a detailed analysis to provide the CEO with insights that will guide business strategy.
The analysis was conducted to determine customer behaviour as well as product purchase performance.
The findings show that there is a generally very good rating, with the average customer rating at 4 out of 5.
The best-selling product, which generated approximately 1.2M INR, is the Cold Brew Coffee Pack.
Aditi Gupta is the store's top customer, both in terms of items purchased and total sales amount.

## Business Problem
Premium Coffee Co is a growing coffee retail brand operating across multiple cities in India.
As the business continues to expand its footprint, the leadership team recognised the need to move away from intuition-based decisions and towards a data-driven approach to strategy.
Without a clear view of how products are performing, which cities are driving revenue, and how customers are behaving, it becomes difficult to allocate resources effectively, identify growth opportunities, and sustain the momentum of expansion. This analysis was therefore commissioned to bridge that gap, providing the CEO with clear, evidence-based insights across sales performance, product behaviour, and customer distribution to guide the next phase of the business.

## Objectives
- What is the total sales generated and the average rating on the products based on customer feedback?
- What are the top best-selling products and which city pulls in the most revenue?
- Who are the customers with the most purchases and total amount spent?
